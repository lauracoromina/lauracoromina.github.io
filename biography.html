Biography

Laura Coromina is a ninth grader currently attending Miami Arts Studio 6-12. She is 14 years old and peruses a job as a civil engineer. She has been taking Technology Production for 3 years and is ahead in math by 2 years. She has done a series of competitions showcasing her talents, such as the MDCPS Refresh Logo Competition and the Innovate CTE Challenge. Laura has participated in theatre production as either production manager or prop handler. Laura has participated in SECME on the Bionic Hand team and in Math Club. She as experience with building things and electrical work. Laura works hard and never gives up. She is determined and tries to produce quality work. She listens and works well with others. Laura's aspiration in life is to have everyone modernized and social with everyone.
